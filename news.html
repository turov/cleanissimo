Tips & tricks

Baking soda

You can also use regular baking soda to remove coffee or tea stains. Wet the area where the stain has formed with warm water. Apply a teaspoon of baking soda to the stain and rub the cloth. Leave the clothes like this for several hours and then rinse them in clean water.

Ammonia + salt solution

70 grams of salt are dissolved in 150 ml of water, the solution is applied to the stain and left for 15 minutes. Remains of chocolate are removed using ammonia. Next, the item is soaked for 5 minutes, after which all that remains is to wash it in the machine on a suitable setting.
